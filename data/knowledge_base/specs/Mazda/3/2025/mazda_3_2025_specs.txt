Mazda 3 (2025) – Technical Specifications

Engine:
- Type: SKYACTIV-G 2.0L (Updated 2025 revision)
- Power: 163 hp
- Torque: 210 Nm
- Fuel system: Direct injection
- Fuel type: Gasoline 95 / E10 compatible
- Emissions standard: Euro 6e

Dimensions:
- Length: 4585 mm
- Width: 1795 mm
- Height: 1455 mm
- Wheelbase: 2720 mm
- Curb weight: 1330 kg

Performance:
- 0–100 km/h: 8.1 seconds
- Transmission: 6-speed automatic (revised shift logic)
- Drive type: FWD
- Fuel consumption: 5.9 L/100km combined

Safety Systems:
- ABS / EBD / BA
- ESC with G-Vectoring Control Plus
- Adaptive Cruise Control (2025 enhanced)
- Lane Keep Assist & Lane Departure Warning
- Blind Spot Monitoring
- AEB with pedestrian detection
- 8 airbags standard

Tire & Wheel:
- Standard tire size: 215/45 R18
- Tire pressure: 34 PSI front / 32 PSI rear
- Spare tire: Space-saver type

Fluids:
- Engine oil: 0W-20 (4.4 L with filter)
- Coolant: Mazda FL22 (Long Life)
- Brake fluid: DOT 4
- Transmission fluid: Mazda ATF FZ

Maintenance Intervals:
- Oil change: every 10,000 km or 12 months
- Air filter: every 25,000 km
- Cabin filter: every 15,000 km
- Spark plugs: every 60,000 km
- Brake fluid: every 2 years

Notes:
- The 2025 model includes mild-hybrid assist on some trims (not all markets).
- Compatible with Mazda Connected Services and OTA updates.
